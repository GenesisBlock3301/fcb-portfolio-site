Who we are

An export-linkage trims & accessories house, built for reliability at scale.

Founded in 2010, FCB Accessories Limited began with corrugated carton, polybag, gum tape and offset printing for its own consumption. Today the company has grown into an 80,000 sq. ft. facility equipped with world-class machinery, run by a team of young and experienced technical, quality and customer-service professionals under dynamic leadership.

We're best known for maintaining consistent quality through an expert QC team — the same discipline that helped us earn certification from OEKO-TEX, FSC and GRS.

  • Fast & reliable lead times — built for the pace of global apparel sourcing.
  • Global Recycled Standard materials — sustainable inputs across product lines.
  • Advanced coating & fusing technology — with custom weight, width & coating options.
